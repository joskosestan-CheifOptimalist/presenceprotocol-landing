Section 1

Proof-of-Presence Consensus

Proof-of-Presence is a new consensus category. Every existing consensus model secures a digital resource — computation, stake, storage, or bandwidth. PoP secures something entirely different: the physical co-presence of human beings in the real world.

Presence Mining is the subsystem through which the network reaches distributed agreement that a verified human encounter occurred. The POP reward is the economic incentive that makes honest participation rational and sustained.

Design Philosophy — Four Balanced Forces

  • Network growth incentives — early participants rewarded generously enough to bootstrap critical mass before utility demand is self-sustaining.
  • Long-term scarcity — supply finite and its schedule credible, creating a defensible store-of-value narrative from day one.
  • Utility demand — POP consumed by real network activity, not merely speculated upon.
  • Fair distribution — rewards flow to genuine human participants, not computational power holders or early insiders.

The Atomic Unit of Value

Understanding what exactly is rewarded is essential for economic credibility. Presence Protocol defines a precise four-stage encounter lifecycle, with a single clearly defined atomic unit at its centre.

Encounter Lifecycle — Four Stages
1
Detected BLE Encounter
Signal proximity confirmed. No mutual signature yet. Tier 1 only.
2
Verified Encounter Ticket
Dual cryptographic signature completed. Valid base encounter.
3
Quality-Scored Encounter Proof
Seven-factor quality score applied. TrustScore multiplier assigned.
4
Relay-Settled Proof
ZK proof posted to Midnight. Reward minted at Cardano L1.
The atomic unit of economic value in Presence Protocol is the quality-scored encounter proof at Stage 3 — a relay-submitted, cryptographically signed encounter ticket that has passed the seven-factor quality scoring system and been assigned a TrustScore multiplier. Stage 1 (detection) and Stage 2 (verification) are prerequisites. Stage 4 (relay settlement) is the reward trigger. Stage 3 is the unit that determines reward magnitude.

Seven Quality Score Inputs — Anti-Collusion Logic

High-Trust status is determined by seven weighted inputs calculated at relay settlement. Together they create a composite quality score that is difficult to game without genuine sustained human interaction.

Dwell Time
25%
Duration of continuous BLE proximity. Minimum 3 minutes for full score. Rapid passing encounters score near zero.
Signal Stability
20%
Consistency of BLE signal strength. Fluctuating or artificially stable signals are flagged as anomalous.
Repeated Confirmations
15%
Number of handshake re-confirmations during the session. Confirms continued active proximity, not just initial detection.
Device Reputation
15%
Historical clean encounter record and wallet reputation score for both parties. New wallets start at neutral; clean history builds over time.
Temporal Uniqueness
10%
Has either device encountered another party in the last 15 minutes? Penalises rapid-fire encounter farming between different parties.
Relay Confidence Score
10%
The relay node's own integrity assessment of the submission. Anomalous submission patterns lower this score automatically.
Anti-Collusion Heuristic
5%
Flags fixed-location patterns and rapidly repeated pair encounters. Detects two devices meeting repeatedly at the same GPS cluster or desk.

Section 7

Adversarial Economics

Sybil resistance is an economic challenge before it is a technical one. The architecture must make attacks unprofitable before they become undetectable. The following analysis establishes attack costs relative to honest participation rewards.

Device Farm Attack
100 fake wallets generating 10 encounters/day each. Requires 50,000 POP minimum stake plus hardware attestation per device. Daily cap of 50 POP/device means over 180,000 fake encounters needed to break even.
✓ Unprofitable at launch economics
Collusion Ring
Two real devices rapidly repeating encounters. 4-hour same-pair minimum, 3 encounter daily maximum, and temporal uniqueness quality scoring all reduce reward to near zero before it becomes profitable.
✓ Caught by quality scoring
Relay Spam
Submitting invalid or duplicate tickets for relay bonus. 500 POP relay stake is fully burned on first detected violation. Single-strike deterrent eliminates the economic incentive completely.
✓ Eliminated by stake risk
Verifier Manipulation
Fraudulently validating fake encounters. 1,000 POP verifier stake slashed proportionally per fraudulent validation. Network reward ceiling is too low relative to stake cost to justify the risk.
✓ High cost, low ceiling

Governance & Cardano Integration

Presence Protocol governance is a three-layer architecture aligned with Cardano's constitutional framework — CIP-1694, Intersect, and the Delegated Representative (DRep) system. This is not generic DAO governance. It positions Presence Protocol as a native participant in the Cardano ecosystem with a credible cross-ecosystem governance bridge.

Layer 1
Constitutional

Immutable — No Vote Can Change These

Total supply cap, halving schedule, PoP cryptographic requirements, minimum BLE validation standard. These form the constitutional layer. Their immutability is what makes the scarcity narrative credible to investors and the network trustworthy to participants.

Layer 2
Protocol DAO

POP Token Holders Vote On

Reward parameters, staking thresholds, burn rates, treasury allocation, quality score weights. All changes subject to timelocks (60–90 days) and quorum requirements (5% of circulating supply). 67% supermajority required for treasury spends above 5M POP.

Layer 3
Cardano Ecosystem

DRep Integration via CIP-1694

POP holders may delegate voting rights to Cardano DReps. 5% of Protocol Treasury allocated to Cardano ecosystem development via Catalyst and Intersect. This builds genuine alignment — not just technical compatibility but financial and political commitment to the Cardano builder community.

The value of Presence Protocol accrues not from what it builds directly, but from what it makes possible for others to build on top of it.
