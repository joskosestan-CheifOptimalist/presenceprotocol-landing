Protocol stack

Four coordinated layers, one participation protocol.

Presence Protocol is not a single mining app. It is a modular infrastructure network that converts physical proximity into verifiable digital participation through encounter capture, validation, relay, privacy, and settlement.

Presence Engine

Deterministic proximity handshakes convert mutual physical presence into signed encounter events.

Encounter Validation

Dual signatures, cooldown rules, and anomaly checks verify that participation is real and not farmed.

Relay Network

Distributed relays propagate encounter receipts for offline-first synchronization and verification.

Settlement & Privacy

Cardano provides transparent settlement while Midnight protects privacy-sensitive participation data.

Participation lifecycle

Deterministic Proof-of-Presence flow.

Scan

Devices discover nearby participants through energy-efficient proximity scanning.

Handshake

Ephemeral keys and dual signatures confirm mutual presence without relying on GPS identity databases.

Queue

Encounter receipts are stored locally so participation remains viable even when devices are offline.

Verify

Relay infrastructure enforces protocol rules, signature validity, cooldown windows, and fraud checks.

Settle

Accepted encounters produce a verifiable participation record eligible for protocol rewards and future applications.

Builder ecosystem

SDKs, reference clients, and infrastructure primitives.

Presence Miner, relay nodes, wallet integrations, and protocol SDKs demonstrate how builders can develop on top of Proof-of-Presence. Applications can use the protocol for participation mining, decentralized reputation, community coordination, and fraud-resistant distribution systems.

SDK & Types

Telemetry Contracts
